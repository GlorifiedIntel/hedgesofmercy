The Rescue Project

This Girl Child Rescue highlights the tragic circumstances faced by Christian girls and families in the Norther regions, including forced marriages, abduction, sexual exploitation, and slavery. The Girl Child Rescue Project seeks to address these issues and provide support to those affected. The following are the categories of rescue:

1. The Girl Child Rescue Project was initiated to rescue girls from impoverished Christian families whose daughters were being abducted and forcibly married to men old enough to be their parents or grandparents as well as forcefully converted to other faiths.
